1. Introduction

At TimeTracking ("we," "our," or "us"), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our TimeTracking Google Workspace Add-on for Google Calendar and any related services (collectively, the "Service").

By accessing or using our Service, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Service:

2.1 Personal Information

When you register for and use our Service, we may collect personally identifiable information, such as:

  • Your name and email address
  • Google account information necessary for authentication
  • Billing information when you subscribe to paid plans
  • User preferences and settings related to the Service

2.2 Calendar Data

Our Service requires access to your Google Calendar data to provide event categorization and time tracking functionality. This includes:

  • Calendar event details (titles, descriptions, times, dates)
  • Categories and labels you create and assign to events
  • Time tracking data derived from your calendar events

2.3 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about how you interact with our Service, including:

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, time and date of visits)
  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Feature usage patterns and preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect about you for various purposes, including:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving the Service

  • Personalizing your experience and delivering content relevant to your interests

  • Protecting the security and integrity of the Service

4. Google API Services User Data Policy

Our use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically, we will:
