Frequently Asked Questions

I already use Google Calendar effectively. Why do I need an add-on?

While Google Calendar is a powerful tool, TimeTracking enhances its functionality by providing unlimited categorization. So far Google Calendar allows to associate color tags to events but the number of options is limited and the data cannot be easily exported for tailored analysis. Timetracking is the tool that allows to get insights that are critical for optimizing your workflow and increasing your efficiency.

I am concerned about the complexity of integrating another tool into my workflow.

TimeTracking is designed for seamless integration with Google Calendar, ensuring that you can start benefiting from its features with minimal disruption to your existing workflow. The intuitive interface makes it easy to adopt and start labeling slots immediately.
